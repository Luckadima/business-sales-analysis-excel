# Business Sales Performance Analysis – Excel Dashboard Project

## Project Overview
This project analyzes business sales data to identify top-performing products, profitable categories, regional performance, and sales trends over time. The goal is to generate business insights and recommendations to help the company improve profitability and sales strategy.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Charts (Bar, Pie, Column, Line)

## Business Questions Answered
1. Which product generates the most revenue?
2. Which category is most profitable?
3. Which region has the highest sales?
4. How do sales change over time?
5. Which products should the business promote?

## Key Insights
- The Technology category is the most profitable.
- The West region generates the highest sales.
- Sales peak during November and December.
- Some furniture products generate high sales but low profit due to discounts.
